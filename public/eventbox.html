Stewardship-A way of living

Stewardship encourages everyone to participate in the task of building the Kingdom of God. God has gifted each of us with everything — our very lives.

We are responsible for caring for these gifts, and accountable for what we do with these gifts. Stewardship rejects the notion that we must “have it all” and, instead, demonstrates the value of giving in love, in service and in justice.

Vocations

PRAYER FOR VOCATIONS

Loving God, You speak to us and nourish us through the life of this church community.

In the name of Jesus, we ask you to send your Spirit to us so that men and women among us, young  and old, will respond to your call to service and leadership in the church.

We pray, especially, in our day, for those who hear your invitation to be a priest, sister, brother, or deacon.

Many those who are opening their hearts and minds to your call be encouraged and strengthened through our enthusiasm in your service. Amen

Christmas Gifts for Children

The St. Vincent de Paul “Giving Tree” is in the vestibule of the church. The tree has ornaments  for girls and boys up to age 13. You can select an ornament and purchase a gift for a child. The  gift does not need to be wrapped. You can leave the gift in the barrel or under the tree until Sunday, December 19th. Thank you for your continued generosity and help in making this Christmas brighter for children in our community. May you have a Merry Christmas!.

Welcome to St. John the Baptist Catholic Church

St. John the Baptist Catholic Church belongs to the Roman Catholic Diocese of Sacramento. The parish church is located in Folsom, California. It was established in 1857. We the people of St. John the Baptist Catholic Church are committed to being an inclusive and welcoming family. We respond through active ministries as a reflection of God’s love and witness to the Gospel in prayer, word, and deed.
