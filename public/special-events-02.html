Special Events 02

Knight's Oktoberfest
Sponsor

Knights of Columbus

When

Sunday, December 12, 2010 at 10 AM, Registration begins at 10 AM

Where

Parish Soccer Field

What

All boys and girls ages 10-14 are invited to participate in the local (council level) competition. Registration begins at 10 a.m. at the "soccer field" which is behind the church and across from the Parish Center.

All competitors must register. Pre-registration is recommended but not required. The registration/entry form can be found on a table in the vestibule of the church. The entry form, copy of birth certificate and parent's signature must accompany participants through all levels of competition. Winners will progress to the district, regional, and state competitions. International champions are chosen on the basis of state-level results.
