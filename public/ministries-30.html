Vocations

The mission of the St. John the Baptist Vocations Committee is to support a prayer campaign for vocations to the priesthood and religious life. The Vocations Committee helps to identify potential candidates, and facilitates our parish Vocations Cross Campaign in effort with Religious Education and SJNDS.

Prayer for Vocations

Loving God, You speak to us and nourish us through the life of this church community. In the name of Jesus, we ask you to send your Spirit to us so that men and women among us, young and old, will respond to your call to service and leadership in the church. We pray, especially, in our day, for those who hear your invitation to be a priest, sister, brother, or deacon. Many those who are opening their hearts and minds to your call be encouraged and strengthened through our enthusiasm in your service. Amen

For those of you considering a vocation

If you or someone you know are considering a Vocation with the Catholic Church, please call our Parish Office (916) 985-2065 to speak with a priest or deacon. Please visit the Diocese of Sacramento website.
