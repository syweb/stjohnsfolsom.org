There is a ministry for everyone at St. John the Baptist Catholic Church and we’re always looking to add new ones to meet the parish needs! Our ministries have been established with you in mind.

Each ministry has been started to bring everyone into a closer relationship with Christ while simultaneously empowering you to make an immediate impact to our parish community and society within we live.

These ministries are available to meet your needs as we worship together, fellowship and reach out to our community in practical ways.

Please visit the individual ministry pages. We offer ministry programs and activities for children through adults.
